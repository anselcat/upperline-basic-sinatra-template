  • Teen Travel Camp at YMCAs of Metro Atlanta

    Travel Camp, aimed at kids ages 12-16, is a fast-paced, action-packed specialty camp designed for the fun-seeking teen who enjoys the outdoors and the adventure of trying new things. Travel Campers will explore an exciting destination every day. Such excursions and activities include hiking, tubing, swimming, bowling, skating, malls, museums, parks, movie theaters, amusement parks and overnight camping trips.
